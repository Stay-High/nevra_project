NEVRA: Personal Pinterest App

Simple personal tool for automated Pinterest pin creation

About the Project

Pinterest Pin Automation is a personal, non-commercial application designed to streamline the process of creating and managing Pinterest pins through the official Pinterest API. The project is intended solely for private use and is not distributed publicly.

Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. This application:

  • Does not collect or store any personal data.
  • Does not share any information with third parties.
  • Operates only with explicit user authorization through the Pinterest OAuth system.
  • Performs actions exclusively on behalf of the authenticated Pinterest account.

All data processing happens locally on the user’s device or within Pinterest’s secure infrastructure.
